This repo contains notes, sketches and ideas about some nicely looking examples of curved 3D spaces (that are not too hard to understand nor they require extensive math skills to understand).

Examples are

- spherical space (take a look at CodeParade youtube demonstration: https://youtu.be/yY9GAyJtuJ0?si=d7F7l9Ij--K4qts-&t=108)
- hyperbolical space (the same YT channel as the previous: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zQo_S3yNa2w)
- conical space - haven't found example of that one yet...
