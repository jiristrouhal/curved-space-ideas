## Plane through the vertex

In the "plane" going through the vertex, either there is seemingly a repetition of objects to the observer (in the case of small cone) or there is a region invisible to the observer (in the case of large cone).

On the left (small cone), both rays from object hit the observer (both sides of the removed region are actually the same line, that's why the ray 1 is seemingly split).

On the right (large cone), there is a blue inserted region, that is "hidden" from the observer. The ray 1 enters the region from left side, passes through it (that's why it is drawn as dashed line there) and exits from behind the vertex. The same applies to the ray 2 entering the hidden region from the right. If the object (cube) was placed to the scene from the left (the same side as the ray 1) and did not yet crossed the dash-dotted line, it is visible to the observer. However, were it moved first from the right and it passed the dash-dotted line for the first time, it is inside the blue region and thus the observer cannot see it.
